🌿 Who We Are

About UGo Greentech

We are a sustainability consultancy committed to shaping a greener built environment through innovative, performance-driven solutions. We support projects in achieving leading certifications including IGBC, GRIHA, EDGE, LEED, CPWD GHAR, ASSOCHAM GEM, ECSBC 2024, ENS 2024, and more.

With expertise in Building Energy Performance Simulations, Urban Heat Island (UHI) Analysis, Daylight & Solar Passive Design Simulations, CFD Analysis, Life Cycle Analysis, and related sustainability assessments, we deliver practical and efficient strategies tailored to each project — ensuring seamless coordination, optimized performance, and sustainable project outcomes.Our services include assessment, feasibility studies, compliance analysis, documentation support, coordination with project teams, design and submission review, site visits, and end-to-end assistance throughout the certification process.

Our Vission and Mission

To become a trusted leader in sustainable development by transforming the built environment through expert consultancy services and environmentally responsible practices. With project experience spanning South Korea, Hanoi, the USA, the Maldives, and across India, we strive to drive sustainability by integrating green building strategies into every project we support, creating a positive impact on communities and the environment while promoting efficient, resilient, and future-ready development.
